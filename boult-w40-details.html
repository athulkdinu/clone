Product Description

The Boult W40 True Wireless Earbuds deliver an exceptional audio experience with advanced Quad Mic Environmental Noise Cancellation technology. Designed and made in India, these earbuds offer an impressive 48 hours of total playback time, making them perfect for extended use throughout your day.

Engineered for gamers, the W40 features an ultra-low 60ms latency gaming mode that ensures perfect audio-video synchronization during intense gaming sessions. The latest Bluetooth 5.3 technology provides stable connectivity and fast pairing with your devices.

With 13mm dynamic drivers, these earbuds deliver rich, detailed sound across all frequencies. The IPX5 water resistance rating makes them suitable for workouts and outdoor activities. Touch controls and voice assistant support add convenience to your daily use.
